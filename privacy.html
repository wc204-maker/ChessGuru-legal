Privacy Policy

Chess Second · Last updated: July 17, 2026

Chess Second (“the App”) is published by Game-ON. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • Chess games and training data are stored primarily on your device.
  • Optional account sign-in syncs some settings and plans to our cloud (Supabase).
  • AI coaching sends chess positions and messages to Google Gemini only when you use those features.
  • Free users may see Google AdMob ads. We may ask for App Tracking Transparency permission for personalized ads.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Information we collect

Account (optional)

If you create an account (email/password, Google, or Sign in with Apple when enabled), we store your email, display name, and authentication tokens via Supabase. You can delete your account in the App under Privacy & Legal.

Chess & training data

Games imported from Chess.com or Lichess, engine games, puzzles, training plans, and progress stats are stored locally on your device. If you are signed in, profile settings, trainer plans, and stats snapshots may sync to Supabase tied to your user ID.

Linked chess accounts

If you link Chess.com or Lichess, we store your username and access token in the iOS Keychain to fetch your games. We do not store your chess platform password.

AI coaching

When you request analysis, chat with the coach, or use voice/blindfold features that rely on AI, relevant chess positions and text are sent to Google Gemini through our Supabase edge functions over HTTPS. Do not include sensitive personal information in chat messages.

Subscriptions & purchases

Payments are processed by Apple. We receive subscription status from StoreKit on your device. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Advertising

The free tier may show ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. On iOS we may request permission to track across apps (ATT); if you decline, you may still see non-personalized ads.

Notifications

Practice reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device. We do not use remote push notifications for marketing.

Diagnostics & usage

We store app preferences and streak data in on-device storage (UserDefaults). We may add anonymous usage or crash diagnostics in future updates; this policy will be updated if that changes.

How we use information

  • Provide training plans, engine play, sync, and AI coaching
  • Maintain your account and subscription entitlements
  • Show ads on the free tier
  • Improve stability and features

Third-party services

  • Apple — App Store, Sign in with Apple, in-app purchases
  • Supabase — authentication and cloud sync
  • Google — Gemini (AI), AdMob (ads)
  • Chess.com / Lichess — public APIs when you link an account

Each provider has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data retention & deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete the App or use “Delete all my data” in Privacy & Legal. Cloud account data is removed when you delete your account in the App.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us using the email below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
